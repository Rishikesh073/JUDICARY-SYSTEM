# LexAgent: Prototype Demonstration Script

### **Scene 1: Introduction & Dashboard (0:00 - 1:00)**
*   **Action**: Start on the **Dashboard**.
*   **Narrative**: "Welcome to LexAgent—your Autonomous Judicial Clerk. We’re looking at the Command Center. As you can see, our engine is connected to a live vault of over **37,000 Supreme Court precedents**. The stats you see here are live—pulled directly from our ChromaDB vector store. LexAgent isn't just a database; it’s a reasoning engine built for 75 years of Indian law."

### **Scene 2: The Live Workspace & Agent Telemetry (1:00 - 2:30)**
*   **Action**: Navigate to **Live Workspace**. Type a complex query (e.g., *"Bail in PMLA cases where no evidence of money laundering is found"*). Click Research.
*   **Narrative**: "Let’s start a research session. Unlike standard AI, LexAgent uses a **Multi-Agent Orchestration**. Watch the telemetry on the right. You can see the **Intent Agent** parsing my query, the **Research Agent** scanning the vector vault, and the **Summarizer** extracting verbatim holdings. 
*   **Action**: Scroll through the streaming memorandum.
*   **Narrative**: "The engine is now synthesizing a court-grade memorandum. It’s not just rephrasing; it’s citing exact legal principles and assigning confidence scores to every precedent found."

### **Scene 3: Relational Intelligence Mapping (2:30 - 3:30)**
*   **Action**: Scroll down to the **Citation Map**. Hover over a node.
*   **Narrative**: "This is our **Relational Intelligence Map**. It visualizes the 'Evolution of Law.' When I hover over a node, the AI tells me the **Selection Reason**—exactly why this case is relevant to my specific query. 
*   **Action**: Hover over a dashed blue line between two nodes.
*   **Narrative**: "These lateral links show citations. If one case cites another, LexAgent maps that relationship instantly, allowing a lawyer to see if they are looking at a binding precedent or a dissenting opinion."

### **Scene 4: The Case Explorer & High-Speed Vault (3:30 - 4:30)**
*   **Action**: Navigate to **Case Explorer**. Filter by Year (e.g., 2020-2025) and Search for "Bail".
*   **Narrative**: "If you want to dive deeper, our **Case Explorer** provides sub-second discovery across our entire 37k dataset. 
*   **Action**: Click on a case card to open the Modal. 
*   **Narrative**: "Inside the case detail, we provide AI-driven insights: Court Holdings, Key Facts, and Significance. But notice the speed—because we use a **Local PDF Streaming Engine**, clicking 'View Original PDF' is instantaneous. No Cloudinary lag, no network latency. It’s built for the speed of a high-stakes courtroom."

### **Scene 5: LexVault & Web3 Security (4:30 - 5:15)**
*   **Action**: Navigate to **LexVault**. Upload a sample document.
*   **Narrative**: "Finally, security. Our **LexVault** uses Web3 technology. When a lawyer finalizes their research, they can 'Secure to Vault.' This pins the document to **IPFS using Pinata**, creating a decentralized, tamper-proof archive of their work."

### **Scene 6: Conclusion (5:15 - 6:00)**
*   **Action**: Return to the **Hero/Landing page**.
*   **Narrative**: "LexAgent combines the privacy of **Local AI (Llama 3.2)**, the transparency of **Agentic Reasoning**, and the security of **Web3**. We are moving Indian Law from 'Search' to 'Intelligence.' Thank you."
